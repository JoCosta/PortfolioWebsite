About Me

Over a career of more than 15 years, I have been a key contributor to different restaurants in several countries.   Starting in my hometown Rome, I have developed and enhanced my skills in restaurants across Italy, Spain, Cayman Islands and London.

As executive chef I conceptualize and develop excellent and innovative menus to optimize guest satisfaction while overseeing comprehensive kitchen operations, including ordering and supply management, food preparation and presentation, special event management, event set-up and effective kitchen staff management ensuring that the best quality of food is served, as well as maintaining a hygienic environment.  I have strong communication and organization skills which I use to engage with the front-house staff, management and marketing team.   I direct and motivate teams effectively to guarantee the success of any dinning establishment.


Eating is a necessity, cooking is an Art

Skills

Experience

Highly accomplished and results-oriented professional, I use resources wisely, developing new menus with different concepts.

I have a vast experience in managing all the kitchen operations and ensuring health and safety standards.

Excellent coaching, developing staff to peak performance levels supervising and coordinating with cooks of all ages and nationalities.

Passion

Always studying and learning to create globally inspired dishes, crafted using only the freshest, locally grown ingredients, fusioning tradition and innovation.

I have absolute commitment in providing the world-class experience clients expect.

Delivering my passion about the food through the dining experience is for me the meaning of being a chef.

Innovation

Diverse culinary experience specializing in Italian, Spanish, French, Japanese and Caribbean cuisine.

I love the idea of getting fresh products and finding different ways to manipulate them to create a tasty and unique dish that is a personal, emotional and multi-sensorial experience.

I like to mix tradition, familiar ingredients and experiment with unique combinations.

Tradition

Having been brought up in Rome, Italy, its huge gastronomic culture is embedded in my cuisine.   My love for food started when I first stepped into my grandma's kitchen to learn her recipes and her art of cooking.

My cuisine is deeply Italian and Mediterranean, sustained in intrinsically modern, well-interpreted traditions that I adapt to satisfy the inspiration gained from living and working in culturally diverse regions around the globe.

PROUD EAST Reopening - LONDON (UK)

Proud East is a popular venue in the vibrant and trendy area of Haggerston, nestled on the banks of the East London Regent's canal. The restaurant and event space reopened its doors in February 2016 with an exceptional menu specially designed for the occasion.   I created a beautifully minimal set of dishes that not only surprised the taste but also the view, being an unexpected pleasure for the senses that also depicted the versatility and contemporary style of the venue itself.
